Gleipnir

On the surface, Gleipnir is a multinational corporation that specialises in the design and production of consumer and military products. The consumer products they develop and produce includes home appliances, furniture, decorative products, and transport vehicles. On the military front, Gleipir produces military vehicles and soldier drones. Beneath the surface, however, Gleipnir serves a more sinister purpose. With the production of military drones, Gleipnir is equipping itself for war, with the eventual goal of taking over the world.

Gleipnir’s various products are created by transforming subjects (mostly unwilling people) into objects through a versatile chemical, nicknamed “Goo”. This Goo is sourced from the Leviathan, a colossal beast living in the center of the Earth.

The Gleipnir Complex

The Gleipnir complex is located in the middle of Velas city. The complex occupies 18.2 km2 (7.0 mi2) of land spaces. The walls separate the Gleipnir space from the rest of Velas, with seven entry points that function as security checkpoints.

Gleipnir Headquarters

Located in the heart of the Gleipnir complex, the Headquarters function as the administrative centre of all of Gleipnir’s operations. There are two areas of the building: the main office and the Underground.

The main office is located above the ground. This is where the central business planning takes place. While most of the floors are occupied by offices, there are recreational amenities such as an art gallery, lounges, and a spa. The facilities are also managed by Gleipnir drones, furniture, and appliances. The radio tower is mounted at the top of Gleipnir Headquarters. The office area holds about 30,000 employees.

The Underground is located below the depth of Level Zero, and is accessible only via the elevators in the Headqaurters. More information about the Underground is available in Appendix A.
